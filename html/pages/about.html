About AES


Aurora Engineering Solutions, Inc. (AES) is a minority-owned Information Technology (IT) services and management consulting company. Founded in 2012, AES’ mission is to support a range of clients by providing customized technology solutions and management consulting services. Passionate and results driven, we apply the most innovative technology and business expertise to conquer our clients’ challenges and moreover, deliver continuously effective value. Equipped with a fresh blend of professional talent, our goal at AES. is to achieve innovative and cost effective technical solutions in concert with our clients


At AES, we strive to incorporate in all we do our guiding principles of excellence, responsibility and integrity. Our commitment to such principles is ingrained in our culture and performance. In our commitment to excellence, our professionals with support of executive team remain dedicated to continued training and development. To promote a culture of responsibility within the company, all AES personnel are held accountable for their work, conduct and commitment to quality. The AES standard of integrity ensures that all client dealings will be forthright, ethical and honest.


AES currently serves the District of Columbia, Maryland, and Virginia areas and is based in Silver Spring, Maryland.
