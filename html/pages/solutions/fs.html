Financial Services

AES' Financial Services Team (FTS) works in tandem with our Federal financial leaders on critical issues of business performance, financial management, accounting, business process improvement, and compliance and fiscal efficiency. Our goal is to assist our clients with developing tailored approaches which help stakeholders enhance decision-making, identify and implement cost reductions, improve internal controls and business processes, and execute on the department's core responsibilities while complying with Federal fiscal policy and regulations and OMB requirements.

AES Solutions Include:

  • Transitioning and implementation of Finance and Accounting Policies
  • Preparation for financial audits and compliance inquiries (audit readiness and support)
  • Internal Control Environment Evaluation
  • Development of Standard Operating Procedure and Policies
  • Financial Analysis, Budgeting, and Reporting
  • Accounting Operations (Accounts Receivable & Payable processing)
