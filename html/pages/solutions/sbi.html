Systems and Business Integration

Our Systems and Business Integration services provide an agile response to clients focused on developing complex systems and subsystems. AES Business Integration staff uses Business Process Mapping (BPM) and Reengineering (BPR) tools to ensure clear understanding of business requirements. Our team also combines proven methodologies from Six Sigma, Project Management Institute (PMI) as well as Information Technology Infrastructure Library (ITIL) to mitigate risk and ensure system solutions to produce visible, measurable results.
