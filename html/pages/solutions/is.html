Information Security

Aurora Engineering Solutions, Inc. (AES) is dedicated to identifying clients' security missions and requirements to develop a security taskforce that is proactive, and resilient. Our team of seasoned professionals works closely with clients to create organization-specific processes and ensure effective implementation of security policy. In addition, information assurance is addressed at every level of each system throughout its life cycle. This includes planning, enterprise controls, design, development, testing, deployment, and operations. Our Security Solutions include but is not limited to:

  • Penetration Testing
  • Audit Logs Review and Reporting
  • Security Administration
  • System Monitoring
  • Certification and Accreditation
  • Compliance Support
