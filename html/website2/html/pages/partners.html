Reseller Services

Aurora Engineering Solutions, Inc. (AES) is a Preferred Partner of Carroll-Net.  Carroll-Net offers a full suite of disaster protection services. Used separately or in combination, these products can help you craft a comprehensive protection plan that is customized for your unique needs.

 

Offsite Data Backup

It is important to protect your data from server crashes, fire, flood and regional disasters. AES through the use of Carroll-Net's Healthy Server system ensures the security, protection and accessibility of all your data during disasters.

 

Hot Site

Surviving disasters requires more than just a good data backup. Companies need a place where they can run their applications if disaster strikes. But typical disaster plans from companies like Sungard, IBM and EDS cost a fortune. Carroll-Net offers alternative plans that are on par with those offered by the big name vendors but cost a fraction of the cost.

 

Disaster Co-Location

For clients who require greater control over their disaster plan, or more frequent updates than available with HotSite, Carroll-Net offers Disaster Co-Location. Carroll-Net's Disaster Co-Location allows clients to store servers at its Datacenter.

 

Network Monitoring

Carroll-Net's  Network Monitoring capabilities provides advance notice when storage is nearing capacity, a rise in temperature in the data closet, or if a client's Internet T1 begins to experience packet loss. This early warning system allows us to help our client's avoid 11th hour rescues by having advance knowledge of impending problems.
